# Voice audit summary

The interface now sounds like Kris building a dangerous little scientific instrument in public with other humans.

It is not a consciousness oracle. It does not flatten disagreement into a score. It shows its evidence, names the human who made the final call, and seals the result with a receipt.

The first mechanical pass found eight issues. The surgical rewrite removed those issues and tightened the real voice failures: vague agency, institutional fog, and bloodless status copy. A final pass across the product, benchmark, roadmap, and research notes returned zero hits. The distinctive language survived because it is carrying product meaning, not merely attitude.
